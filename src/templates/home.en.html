The Pipeline

One line, from sketch to engine

Nine stages, each one continuing the last, each handing you a result you can check. The slow, automatable work goes to AI; the craft that truly makes or breaks it, BlueCat trains you on.

04Retopo & OptimizationBlueCat-coached

Prep for texturing. Auto-unwrap gets you started; seams / islands / material cleanup need hand work.

The pattern that emerges

The difficulty all clusters in the middle (Sculpting 03 / Retopo 04 / Rigging 08) — the loose ends AI can't tie up are exactly where BlueCat trains you. The two ends (generation, export) are actually the easiest.

FAQ

Still on the fence? Answers here

+Do I need any 3D or coding background?
No. The course is built for absolute beginners and explains every step from scratch. If you already have some background, you'll move faster and dig deeper.
+What computer specs do I need?
Any reasonably recent computer that can run Blender and basic AI tools is enough. A dedicated GPU is recommended but not required.
+How much do the tool subscriptions cost?
Most tools used in the course are free or come with a generous free tier. Optional paid tiers usually run around $10–30 a month.
+How long does it take to finish?
The content is equivalent to a 5-day structured course. Most people finish in about a week, then revisit the modules as reference while working on their own projects.
+Can I take on paid work after this?
Yes. The whole course is designed to get you from zero to a portfolio that attracts real clients.
